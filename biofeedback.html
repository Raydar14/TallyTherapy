What biofeedback is

Biofeedback uses sensors to show you what your body is doing in the moment — signals like breathing, heart rate, and muscle tension. By watching these signals change in real time, you can practice calming techniques and see what's working, building skills you can use long after the session ends.

It's often paired with talk therapy to support stress, anxiety, and tension, and to strengthen the mind–body connection.

Often used to support

  • Stress and anxiety regulation
  • Physical tension and relaxation
  • Greater mind–body awareness
  • Coping skills you keep for life
