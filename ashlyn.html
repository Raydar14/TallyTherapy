Therapeutic focus

As a counselor intern in the state of Florida, I provide therapeutic services with a client-centered and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) approach. My focus is on college-age women — helping them navigate trauma, move through life transitions, build healthier habits, and improve social skills. I integrate warm empathy and virtual reality to create a supportive environment for growth.

As a Certified Mental Performance Consultant® (CMPC) through the Association of Applied Sport Psychology (AASP), I also use mental-performance strategies to help both athletes and non-athletes strengthen their resilience and well-being. Together, we'll identify areas for growth and work toward your personal and therapeutic goals.
