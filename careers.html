We are hiring licensed therapists

Powers of Mind Psychological Services is a private psychology practice in midtown Tallahassee. We're looking for clinicians who want a supportive, low-overhead place to see clients in person — with the freedom to build their own schedule.

Who we're looking for

  • Licensed Psychologists
  • Licensed Mental Health Counselors
  • Post-Doctoral Residents

Why join us

Set your own hours, keep your documentation simple, and focus on the work that matters — meeting clients face to face in a calm, welcoming space. Whether you're looking for part-time or full-time, we'll build a schedule that fits your life.

The details

  • Set your own hours — part-time or full-time
  • In-person sessions in midtown Tallahassee
  • $50–$125 per therapy hour
  • Cash-pay practice — no insurance, simple documentation
