Therapeutic focus

I offer therapeutic services to individuals and couples with a client-centered and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) approach. In our sessions, we'll collaborate to identify the areas where you most want to grow.

I specialize in working with men facing issues related to porn addiction, unhealthy behaviors, and phobias, using virtual-reality techniques and group therapy for support. My goal is to create a steady, judgment-free space where real change feels possible.
