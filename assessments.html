What an assessment provides

A psychological assessment uses structured interviews and validated testing to answer a defined question — whether that's a diagnosis, an accommodation, or a treatment recommendation. You receive a clear, written report you can act on.

Evaluations are tailored to the referral question. Tell us what you need and we'll tell you if we can help, what it will involve, and what it will cost.

How it works

1

Referral & scope

We clarify the question, timeline, and what the evaluation must address.

2

Interview & testing

Structured clinical interview plus validated instruments selected for the question.

3

Written report

A clear, defensible report with findings and, where appropriate, recommendations.
